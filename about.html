About Us

Empowering RTU students with free academic resources

Our Mission

Welcome to RTU CS/IT Notes, the ultimate platform designed specifically for B.Tech Computer Science and Information Technology students of Rajasthan Technical University.

Our goal is to provide high-quality, easily accessible, and mobile-friendly handwritten notes for all 8 semesters. We understand the challenges of slow internet and minimal mobile devices, which is why our platform is incredibly lightweight and fast-loading.

What We Offer

  • Free Unit-wise PDF Notes for all semesters
  • Previous Year Question Papers
  • Viva & Important Questions
  • Quick Revision Guides
